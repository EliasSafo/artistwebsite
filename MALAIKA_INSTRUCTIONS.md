## 🎵 Adding a New Song/Album

1. **Go to Admin Panel** → Click "Music Releases"
2. **Click "New Music Release"**
3. **Fill in the details:**
   - **Title:** Name of the song/album
   - **Type:** Choose Single, Album, or EP
   - **Release Date:** Pick from calendar
   - **Cover Art:** Drag & drop your album cover image
   - **Spotify Link:** Copy/paste from Spotify
   - **Apple Music Link:** Copy/paste from Apple Music
   - **YouTube Link:** Copy/paste from YouTube
   - **Tracklist:** (Optional) Add track names for albums
4. **Click "Publish"**
5. **Done!** It appears on your Music page

### Getting Your Streaming Links:

**Spotify:**
- Open your song/album on Spotify
- Click "..." (three dots)
- "Share" → "Copy Song/Album Link"
- Paste into CMS

**Apple Music:**
- Same process - share and copy link

**YouTube:**
- Copy the URL from your YouTube video

## 📸 Uploading Images

### Image Guidelines:
- **Album Covers:** Square (1000x1000px or larger)
- **Press Photos:** Landscape (1920x1080px recommended)
- **Merch Photos:** Square (800x800px or larger)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 5MB for fast loading

### How to Upload:
1. Click the image field in any form
2. Drag & drop your image OR click to browse
3. Image uploads automatically
4. You'll see a preview

## ⚠️ Important Things to Know

### Publishing vs Saving
- **Save Draft:** Saves but doesn't publish (not visible on site)
- **Publish:** Makes it live on your website

### How Long Do Changes Take?
- 2-3 minutes after clicking "Publish"
- The site needs to rebuild
- Refresh your website to see updates

### Editing Existing Content

4. Click "Publish"

### Deleting Content
